    Research on Image-based Localization

    Reaserch Assistant, supervised by Prof. Hesheng Wang, Autonomous Robot Lab | 2019.12 - 2020.02

    In this project, we proposed a fairly new model to implement the image-based localization task. We firstly obtained depth prediction map and semantic segmentation map from the original RGB image through an encoder-decoder structure. The two maps generated were then used to do the image retrieval process to find the most similar scene to the RGB image. Specifically, a multi-scale reconstruction loss and multi-scale triplet loss were built to train the network. We trained our model on virtual-KITTI dataset and tested on the Extended CMU Seasons dataset. Benchmark URL: https://www.visuallocalization.net/benchmark/

    Research on Multi-object Tracking and Instance Segmentation

    Reaserch Assistant, supervised by Prof. Ming Yang, CyberC3 Intelligent Vehicle Lab | 2020.04 - Present

    In this project, we integrated the advantages of both multi-object tracking and instance segmentation, designed and implemented a new multi-object tracking and instance segmentation method to achieve a satisfactory accuracy, so as to achieve a fine-grained instance segmentation effect while ensuring the tracking results of multiple frames. We tested our model on KITTI dataset. URL: http://www.cvlibs.net/datasets/kitti/eval_mots.php
